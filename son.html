Explore Sonmarg - The Meadow of Gold

Experience the breathtaking landscapes, stunning glaciers, and exciting activities that Sonmarg has to offer throughout the year.

Top Attractions in Sonmarg

    Famous Glaciers

    Sonmarg is home to some majestic glaciers, ideal for trekking and exploration.

    Thajiwas Glacier

    One of the most popular glaciers in Sonmarg, offering incredible views and adventure-filled treks.

    Zero Point Glacier

    At the highest point of Zoji La Pass, Zero Point offers a surreal icy landscape even in summer.

    Zero Point and Union Cab Rides

    Explore the famous Zero Point through the local Union Cab services, which offer a scenic drive through the snow-clad mountains.

    Cab services to Zero Point are managed by the local Union, ensuring a safe and comfortable journey through breathtaking landscapes.

    • Cab fare: Approx. ₹3000 - ₹4000 (Sedan)
    • Duration: 3 hours (round trip)

    Fish Point & Gateway of Ladakh

    Fish Point

    Sonmarg’s Fish Point is perfect for relaxing by the river and indulging in delicious local trout.

    Gateway to Ladakh

    Located at Zoji La, Sonmarg serves as the gateway to Ladakh, with its stunning views and historical significance.

    Sonmarg in Summer & Winter

    Each season brings its own beauty and activities to Sonmarg.

    Summer Season

    In summer, Sonmarg is a lush meadow, perfect for picnics, treks, and camping under clear blue skies.

    Winter Season

    In winter, Sonmarg transforms into a snowy paradise with opportunities for snowboarding, skiing, and ice skating.

    Krishnasar and Vishansar Lakes

    These twin high-altitude lakes are famous for their stunning beauty and trekking trails. Surrounded by lush greenery in summer, the lakes offer pristine landscapes, making them a favorite for photographers and nature lovers alike.

    Trekking: The trek to these lakes is moderately challenging and provides breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and meadows.

    Activities: Fishing, camping, and photography are popular activities around these lakes.

    Baltal: The Gateway to Amarnath Yatra

    Baltal, located just 15 km from Sonmarg, is famous for being the base camp for the annual Amarnath Yatra. It also offers camping options and panoramic views of the Himalayan range.

    Activities at Baltal

    • Trekking to Amarnath Cave
    • Camping by the Sind River
    • Horse riding

    Satisfy your cravings at some of the best eateries in Sonmarg.

    Sonmarg Canteen

    A cozy spot offering a variety of local dishes, including traditional Wazwan meals.
