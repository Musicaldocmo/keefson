Fishing

Top Tourist Attractions

Aru Valley

A serene valley surrounded by lush greenery and snow-capped peaks.

Baisaran

Also known as 'Mini Switzerland,' this meadow offers breathtaking views of the valley.

Betaab Valley

Famous for its Bollywood connections, Betaab Valley is a picturesque destination.

Travel Tips and Important Information for Pahalgam

Pahalgam is known for its scenic beauty, but there are a few things you should know before you go. Here are some essential tips to make your trip smoother:

Weather & Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Pahalgam is from March to November. Winters can be harsh, so pack accordingly.

Clothing Tips

Carry warm clothes even in summer as evenings can get chilly. Don't forget waterproof shoes if you plan to trek.

Medical Information

Pahalgam is a hilly area. Carry basic medications, especially if you are prone to altitude sickness.

Respect Local Culture

Always respect the local customs and traditions. Dress modestly and be mindful of the local sensitivities.
